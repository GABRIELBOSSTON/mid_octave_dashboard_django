    Methodology

    This assessment follows the OCTAVE Allegro v1.0 (Operationally Critical Threat, Asset, and Vulnerability Evaluation) methodology, developed by Carnegie Mellon University's Software Engineering Institute. OCTAVE Allegro is a streamlined, risk-based approach focused on information assets and their exposure in operational environments.

    Steps Followed
    1. Establish Risk Measurement Criteria (WS-1 to WS-6)
    2. Develop Information Asset Profiles (WS-8)
    3. Identify Asset Containers (WS-9)
    4. Identify Areas of Concern (WS-10)
    5. Identify Threat Scenarios (WS-10)
    6. Identify Risks (WS-10)
    7. Analyse Risks — Score = Impact × Priority (WS-10)
    8. Select Mitigation Approach (WS-10)
